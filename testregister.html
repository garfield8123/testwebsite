About this course

The event is a 24-hour hackathon or coding competition where high school students can create whatever they would like from simple HTML websites to complex video games or apps. Participants can win prizes after review from a panel of judges. This event is FREE. There will be free food, free drinks, games and more prizes. Participants must bring their own laptops, sleeping bag, pillow and toiletries. This is a 'lock-in' event. Participants will not be dismissed until 12 p.m. February 16, 2020 or unless their parents or guardians come an pick them up.
